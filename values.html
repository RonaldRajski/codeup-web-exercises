Codeup's Core Values

These are extremely important to us. These core values are the foundation of how we do business, how we conduct ourselves and how we interact with each other. We look to these core values as guidance when making decisions and developing strategies to fulfill our mission.

What do these values mean to you?


When I was in the Navy, I learned about the core values Honor, Courage and Commitment. These replaced the code I tried to live as an Eagle Scout a year before joining the Navy.

Be Helpful

What does "Be Helpful" mean to you?


Be Helpful means to assist others when they need help, but one needs to remain careful not to do the job for that person, and must give the opportunity to complete the task or fail the task.

Cultivate Inclusive Growth

What does "Cultivate Inclusive Growth" mean to you?

"Cultivate Inclusive growth" means to give others on your team the freedom to struggle through a process and intervene at intervals when appropriate. One should provide the tools needed for a team to successfully complete a project and then step back and faciltate the project.

Deliver Excellence

What does "Deliver Excellence" mean to you?

Deliver Excellence means to give 110% everyday so that on bad day you can give %100. It's cliche but it is true

Act with Integrity

What does "Act with Integrity" mean to you?


Act with Integrity" means to do the right thing even when no one is looking.
